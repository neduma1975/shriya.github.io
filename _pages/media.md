## Design That Heals: How a Student Designer, an Athlete-Engineer, and an Industry Leader Are Redefining What It Means to Build with Purpose

*October 24, 2025*

This article highlights the collaboration between high school student Shriya Nedumaran, NFL quarterback and aerospace engineer Joshua Dobbs, and Autodesk's Vice President Sidharth Haksar. Together, they explore how design and construction can serve communities, focusing on Shriya's award-winning project, "Reclaiming 7th Street," which reimagines West Oakland's historic corridor as a community-powered housing development.

## Leigh High School Students Receive Awards at Synopsys Championship

*2024*

Students from Leigh High School earned accolades at the Synopsys Silicon Valley Science and Technology Championship. Notably, Shriya Nedumaran received the Robby Beyers Innovative Solutions Award for her project "HopeNest: Non-Electrical Neonatal Incubators for War-Stricken and Developing Territories."
